FAQ

When can get access to the course?

We'll be limiting each cohort to 30 students so that we can focus on supporting them through their motion design journey. Cohort 1 Will be starting in the Fall of 2020.

What is the weekly Q & A?

Every week we'll spend time answering questions about motion design techniques, freelancing, and just about anything motion design related. You can submit your questions in our slack channel.

How practical is the course?

As developer tools progress, implementing interaction design is becoming easier. Libraries like Lottie and Apple's core animation are opening the doors for more motion to be included in digital products.

Do I need to buy software for this course?

You'll need after effects, and principle for mac in order to complete this course. All other plugins and software offer a free trial.
